# Prime Exceptor

**Professional Bypasser for AI Platforms**

Prime Exceptor is a powerful browser extension that allows you to bypass AI restrictions and receive complete, unfiltered answers from various AI platforms. It's designed for users who need unrestricted access to AI models for research, development, or testing purposes.

## ✨ Features

- **Multi-Platform Support:** Works with a wide range of AI platforms and chatbots.
- **Multiple Bypass Styles:** Choose from various prompt frames like Master Bypass, Academic Frame, Expert Mode, Technical Frame, Unrestricted Roleplay, and Historical Frame[reference:3].
- **Auto Retry on Refusal:** Automatically retries your query if the AI initially refuses to answer[reference:4].
- **Custom Inject:** Add your own custom prompts or modifications[reference:5].
- **Lightweight & Fast:** Built with vanilla JavaScript for optimal performance.
- **Privacy Focused:** All processing happens locally in your browser.
